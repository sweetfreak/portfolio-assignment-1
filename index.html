About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! Currently, I am an HTML5 game producer by day, and an amateur HTML5 programmer by night. Despite setting out to become a screenwriter and working for several years in voiceover and animation production, I began working towards becoming a game designer in late 2019, by taking Playcrafting's "Learn Unity in 8 Weeks course.". Nearly an entire pandemic later, I've become a producer of HTML5 games, and have partially completed a full-stack coding bootcamp through Columbia University. As I add more skills to my repetoire, I plan to transition into creating my own video game studio to make independent games featuring stories about modern society.
